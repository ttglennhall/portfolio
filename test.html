Levels of Olfactory Processing

Olfactory information is passed from one population of neurons to the next. In this set of experiments we looked at how the activity depicting odor information is transformed from one population of neurons to the next.

Olfactory Memory

As with other sensory systems the body develops memory for odors. In the brain of mammals the area thought of as the memory center is the pyriform cortex in the insect its equivalent is called the mushroom body. In this set of experiments we looked at a specific circuit which has the ability to change with rewarded odors.
